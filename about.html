What is SAND?

SAND is a genre agnostic tabletop RPG meaning, unlike many other alternatives, SAND is not exclusively fantacy or Sci-Fi.

SAND was created with the purpose of making it easy to create whatever setting or character you'd want. Because of this, you will find creating your own classes, items, and abillities to be a straight-forward process.
