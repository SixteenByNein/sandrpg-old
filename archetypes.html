Archetypes

Rogue:

You don't always follow the rules, instead you follow your own path. You may act aloof or disinterested. As much as you'd like others to believe, you are not a master of all trades.


Characters of this archetype include: Han Solo, Indiana Jones, and basically any Humphrey Bogart character.

Wizard:

You're highly respected for your wisdom and are likely older and more experienced than the rest of the characters. Chances are you are a supporting character in the the story. You do not neccesarily have magic abilities.


Characters of this archetype include: Gandalf, Dumbledore, Ben Kenobi, Yoda, and Mr. Miyagi.

Vigilante:

You take justice into your own hands. You likely have superhuman powers or advanced technology. You may feel that your actions are justified but critics may question your brutal tactics or lack of accountibility from the government or your fellow citizens.


Characters of this archetype include: Batman, Spider-Man, and Robin Hood.

Everyman:

In a word you are mundane. You don't really fit in with the more competent or cool characters you're around. You may be the reason thing are explained to the party that most of the characters would already know.


Characters of this archetype include: Arthur Dent, Doctor Watson, Bilbo Baggins, and Emmet Brickowski.
